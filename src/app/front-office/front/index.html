What happens if I pay and the seller fails to approve me on time?

Make a report to the administration with the necessary screenshots to attest to the transaction. The management will take steps to verify the transaction at the operator's premises and take the necessary measures.

You have 5 hours to make the payment. If you do not, your account will be automatically blocked in the system with all your coins.
